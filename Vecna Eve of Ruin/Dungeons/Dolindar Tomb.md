*Encountered in [[Evernight]] in [[Ch. 1]] quest [[Escape Evernight]]*

## General Features
- **Unending isolation:** Creatures can feel the isolation and suffering in the tomb cause creatures to be unable to take the Help action while inside.
- **Stone construction:** The tomb is made of old durable stone.
- **Lighting:** The tomb is dark. Characters need either a light source or some other method to see in the dark.
- **Doors:** The heavy doors throughout the tomb are made of stone and grind noisily when open and shut. No doors are locked except for the puzzle door in are B5.
- **Ceilings:** Ceilings are 10 feet high throughout the tomb.


## History
The tomb of the deceased Dolindars. 

## Plot relevance
- The players escape [[Evernight]] through the stable crevice of dusk found here.
