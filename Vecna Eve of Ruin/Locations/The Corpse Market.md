
## Description
Everything in the market exhibits pale, subdued colors, but the atmosphere is lively as ghouls, skeletons, and vampires meander from stall to stall.

## The Market's Goods
- Ghoulish remains intended for consumption.
- Shops sell boquets of dead flowers, frayed burial finery, jewelry displayed on severed hands, elegant canopic jars and the like.
## Market Clientele
 Occasionally sees living visitors, though the characters are the only ones present now. If they not try to disguise themself tehy recieve sidelong stares from merchants and customers. 
 Everyone assumes the characters are there under the assumption the characters would not be here unless they were under the protection of an influential figure in the city or very powerful travelers in their own right.
