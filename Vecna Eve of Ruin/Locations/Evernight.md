*An echo of [[Neverwinter]] in [[The Shadowfell]], encountered in [[Ch. 1]] quest [[Escape Evernight]]*


## Plot Relevance
- The characters get transported here after stopping the cults ritual and need to find a way back to [[Neverwinter]]. 
- They meet [[Sangora]] at [[The Corpse Market]] and are led to the [[Dolindar Tomb]].

## Description
[[Neverwinter]]'s dismal opposite. The sun never shines on Evernight, and ash-laden fog raises from lava flowing through the city in the place of Neverwinters River, choking the city.
Evernight is populated by Undead - primarly vampires and ghouls - who pray on each other and travelers. 
The city is a crossroads of trade in [[The Shadowfell]] and host numerous markets, including [[The Corpse Market]].

## NPCs found here
- [[Sangora]] the vampire merchant
- [[Newmy]] a ghost in [[Dolindar Tomb]]
