*An echo of [[Neverwinter]] in [[The Shadowfell]], encountered in [[Ch. 1]] quest [[Escape Evernight]]*


## Plot Relevance
- The characters get transported here after stopping the cults ritual and need to find a way back to [[Neverwinter]].

## Description
[[Neverwinter]]'s dismal opposite. The sun never shines on Evernight, and ash-laden fog raises from lava flowing through the city in the place of Neverwinters River, choking the city.
