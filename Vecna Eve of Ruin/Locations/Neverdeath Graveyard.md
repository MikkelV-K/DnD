## Description
A graveyard in [[Neverwinter]] split into two connected sections by a thick stone wall. The Main Graveyard and the Pauper's Graveyard. The Main graveyard hols several mausoleums, some with expansive underground chambers. The Pauper's Graveyard features numerus simple headstones, but a few civic citizens funded communal catacombs when the graveyard were first built.

## Plot Relevance
- The trail of a kidnapped noble leads to the Hallix Mausoleum, the entrance to [[The Neverdeath Catacombs]].
