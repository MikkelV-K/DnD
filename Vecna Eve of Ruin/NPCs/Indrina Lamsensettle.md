*First encountered in [[Ch. 1]] and relevant in [[Save the Kidnapped Nobles]]*


## Role
Kidnapped noble in [[Neverwinter]] which has dirt on [[Dagult Neverember]].

## First encountered
Indrina can be found in a cell in the catacombs. She is not happy with her accomodations, especially the smell. If the characters reveal that they work [[Dagult Neverember]] Indrina does not reveal what she knows. If they do not disclose this and ask her why she is here they can make a DC 18 Charisma (Persuation) check. On a succsess she reveals her secret.

## Details
A human actor who moves in Neverwinter's highest social circles.


## Secret
She has proof [[Dagult Neverember]] does not have a legitimate claim to his title, he is not related to Lord Nashar Alagondar as he claims.
