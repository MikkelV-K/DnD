*First encountered in [[Ch. 1]] and relevant in [[Save the Kidnapped Nobles]]*

## Role
Kidnapped noble in [[Neverwinter]] which has had a disturbing vision.

## First Encounter
Sarcelle is found in a cell in the Neverdeath Catacombs. Interacting with Sarcelle and succeeding on a DC 14 Wisdom(Insight) check realizes something is bothering the sorcerer. Asking about it makes her share her vision.

## Details
A human wild-magic sorcerer who plumbs the mysteries of the Outer Planes.

## Secret
Sarcelle saw a dreadful future. She saw the desiccated figure of a man levitating off the ground, gathering evil energy around himself in glowing wisps. He then screamed and then exploded, causing something terrible to happen. This terrified Sarcelle.
