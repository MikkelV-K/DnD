*First encountered in [[Ch. 1]]* and relevant in [[Save the Kidnapped Nobles]]

## Role
An enemy of the characters, he is the "Thought of Vecna" as the cult leader of this part of [[The Cult of Vecna]].

## First Encounter
Jerot is refining the ritual occurring in the Sacrifice Gallery and is to focused to be distracted by suspicious sound elsewhere. If the characters enter his chamber he commands the nothic to enter melee while attacks at a distance, summoning Undead defenders if the nothic fails. He fights to the death.

## Details
A neutral evil [[Necromancer Wizard]]. The leader of a section of the cult of Vecna presiding in the Neverdeath Catacombs. Jerot is an aristocrat who has lived his entire life in [[Neverwinter]]. He has built his deep faith in Vecna and vast necromantic knowledge over many years, right under the nose of his peers. At the moment of first encounter his friends and family believe him to be on an extended trip to Waterdeep. He considers his secret life as another to yet another way of honoring his evil patron. 

## Jerot's Papers
Notes describing the ritual, tests where cultist have been used. He explain he has chosen nobles who he believe to have particulary meaningful secrets. A DC 14 Intelligence(Investigation) reveals some notes about a magical phenomena called "Crevises of Dusk". The notes indicate som connection bewteen the ritual and a plane populated by the Undead. He intends to learn more.
