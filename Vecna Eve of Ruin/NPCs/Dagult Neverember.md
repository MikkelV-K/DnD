*First encountered in [[Ch. 1]] gives the quest [[Save the Kidnapped Nobles]]*
## Role
Former Open Lord of Waterdeep; current  Lord Protector of [[Neverwinter]]. 

## First Impression
- Proud
- Defensive about his legacy
- Powerful
- Recognition
- Likes to drink

## What He Wants
- To protect Neverwinter
- To profit when possible
- To survive [[Vecna]]'s schemes intact

## What He knows
- Fragmentary information about [[Vecna]]'s plans
- Name of factions involved
- Knows more than he admits
## What He Hides
- Fear that [[Vecna]] could destabilize his rule
- He is not in fact descended from one of [[Neverwinter]]'s past heroes.
