## Role
Kidnapped noble in [[Neverwinter]] who earlier was a historian on Vecna.


## First encounter
Umberto can be found in a cell in the Neverdeath Catacombs. He keeps complaining about the poor cuisine to keep his mind of the situation. If the characters share some tasty food with him he reveals his expertise in Vecna's history. After revealing this it is all he can talk about. He then disusses his latest project: the ancient rivalry between Kas and Vecna. He knows he is a slow writer,

## Details
A gnome historian who has written books on various deities.

## Secret
Umberto is scared to admit he is an expert on Vecna's history.
