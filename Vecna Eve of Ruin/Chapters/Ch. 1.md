# Return from Neverdeath Graveyard

In this chapter the charcters are sent on a mission to find four kidnapped nobles. Their traces lead to the entrace of the Hallix Mausoleum, which is the entrance to [[The Neverdeath Catacombs]] Here [[The Cult of Vecna]] has captured the nobles to do rituals on them to steal their secrets. The characters stop the ritual and gets transported to [[Evernight]] while being linked to [[Vecna]]. They then  they escape through a crevice of dusk in the [[Dolindar Tomb]].
